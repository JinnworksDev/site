Privacy Policy - Is There Escape?

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jinnworks ("we," "our," or "the creator") handles information when you play Is There Escape? ("the Game"). We value your privacy and believe in keeping data collection as minimal as possible.

1. Information We Collect Directly

The Game does not require any account registration or personal details (such as your name or email address) to play. We only collect basic, non-personally identifiable gameplay data to help improve the game experience. This includes:

  • Total time spent playing
  • In-game progression and achievement milestones
  • Basic gameplay metrics (such as crash frequency or error logs)
2. Third-Party Services & Game Engine Telemetry

The Game is built using the Unity game engine. Unity may automatically collect certain device-specific information, such as your IP address, device hardware specifications, operating system, and unique device identifiers to ensure game performance and stability. For more information on how Unity handles this data, please review the Unity Privacy Policy directly on their official website.

Additionally, because the Game is distributed through storefronts like Steam and Itch.io, those platforms may collect purchase history and usage statistics governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. How We Use the Data

Any basic gameplay or performance data collected is strictly used to identify technical bugs, balance game mechanics, and optimize performance across different hardware configurations. We do not sell, trade, or share your information with outside marketers.

4. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal identifiers, we do not store personal profiles. Local save files and progress logs reside on your personal device and can be deleted by removing the Game or clearing local app data.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game does not intentionally or knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe any such data has inadvertently been transmitted, please contact us so we can verify and remove it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any revisions will be updated directly on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how data is managed, please contact us via our official support channels or linked community profiles.
