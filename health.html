HEALTH & CONTENT WARNING

Critical Health & Content Warning

Last updated: July 5, 2026

PLEASE READ THIS NOTICE CAREFULLY BEFORE PLAYING.
Is There Escape? is an extreme psychological survival horror experience. By proceeding, you acknowledge that you are aware of the following intense visual effects, gameplay mechanics, and mature themes.

1. Photosensitivity, Strobe, and Motion Warning

The Game utilizes intense visual design choices that may trigger discomfort or adverse medical reactions. This includes:

  • Severe Visual Effects: Continuous flashing lights, sudden blindingly bright lights, rapid visual distortion effects, and intense strobe-like sequences.
  • Screen & Window Manipulation: Extreme camera shake, aggressive screen jitter, and intentional manipulation/shaking of the actual application/game window interface.

If you have a history of epilepsy, seizures, severe motion sickness, or vertigo, do not play this game. Discontinue play immediately if you experience dizziness, disorientation, or eye twitching.

2. Audio and Shock Hazard

The sound design includes sudden, highly elevated loud noises, jarring audio shifts, and sudden jump scares designed to elicit a severe startle response. Player discretion is advised if you have a heart condition, high blood pressure, or anxiety disorders.

3. Graphic Violence, Blood, and Gore

The Game depicts explicit, uncompromised graphic violence. Players will encounter:

  • Copious amounts of blood and gore.
  • Detailed corpses and severed limbs/dismemberment.
  • Grotesque hostile monsters and horrific depictions of death.
4. Mature Themes and Psychological Triggers

The narrative features deeply unsettling themes intended solely for mature audiences:

  • Suicide: The Game contains explicit narrative mentions and direct visual depictions of suicide. If you are sensitive to these themes, please refrain from playing.
  • Substance Use: Features the active in-game usage of alcohol and drugs, accompanied by severe, disorienting visual and mechanical gameplay effects.
  • Crude Language: Contains pervasive strong, explicit language throughout.
5. Safe Practices

If you still choose to play, please do so while taking frequent breaks. Your mental and physical well-being should always come first.
