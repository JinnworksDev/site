Health & Content Warnings

Is There Escape? is a horror game and contains content that may be distressing to some players. Please review the warnings below before playing.

Photosensitivity

This game contains flashing lights and rapid visual effects that may trigger seizures for people with photosensitive epilepsy. Player discretion is advised.

Jumpscares & Sudden Loud Noises

This game includes jumpscares and sudden loud sound effects intended to startle the player.

Graphic Violence & Gore

This game depicts graphic violence and gore, including injury and death.

Monstrous Creatures

This game features frightening, monstrous creatures and hostile encounters throughout.

Substance Use

This game contains depictions of substance use.

⚠ Suicide — References & Depictions

This game contains references to and depictions of suicide. Please take care while playing, and skip this game if this is not something you wish to engage with.

If you or someone you know is struggling, the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988 in the US) is available 24/7.
